GreenPad Concepts

Supporting girls and women in Nigeria to stay in school

Support

GreenPad Concepts harness the potential of Nigeria school girls through sensitization and counseling programme on puberty stage, menstruation and menstrual hygiene. For rural and semi-urban school girls in Nigeria, GreenPad Concepts helps keep them in class during their menstrual period to get education, better than missing 4 – 5 classes a month and later drop out of school.

GreenPad Concepts educates school girls on puberty stage, menstruation and menstrual hygiene with our sensitization programme and counselling and we manufacture bio-degradable, low-cost and accessible sanitary pads as oppose using unhygienic materials and expensive products.
